Company Overview

Surveillance World was founded in 2020 with the vision of providing reliable, innovative, and high-quality electronic security solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional clients across Odisha.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted technology and security solutions provider with the support of our experienced engineers and dedicated technical team having 10+ years of industry experience in electronic security and networking systems.

Today, Surveillance World proudly serves hundreds of satisfied customers across Odisha by delivering professional installation, genuine products, and dependable after-sales support.
